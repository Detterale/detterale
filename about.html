About me

Hi, I'm Danil, born and raised in Shymkent, Kazakhstan. Music has been a part of my life for as long as I can remember. I started getting into music as a kid, and my passion grew when I joined a music school. Over the years, I’ve honed my skills not just on guitar, but also on bass guitar, keyboard, and drums.

I've had the privilege of performing in concerts, sharing my love for music on stage. After studying music formally, I went on to teach guitar and bass at the "Groove" Music School, helping others find their voice in the music world.

With my background in multiple instruments, years of experience, and a focus on songwriting, I bring a unique perspective to my reviews and insights. Whether you're just starting out or looking to deepen your understanding of music, I'm here to share what I've learned on this incredible journey.

Songwriting is another passion of mine, and I love the creative process of crafting lyrics and melodies. Currently, I’m part of a band called Diseased Convergence, where I play bass. We’re gearing up for a concert on October 18, and I can’t wait to share our music with the audience!
